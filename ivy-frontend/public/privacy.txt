Privacy Policy

Last updated: 24 August 2025

1. Data We Collect

We collect minimal data from users:

    IP Addresses: For analyzing traffic patterns and security purposes.
    Solana Network Data: Cached to optimize read calls and reduce compute costs.
    Analytics Data: We use third-party analytics services to collect anonymized usage data including:
        - Page views and navigation patterns
        - Browser type and device information
        - Referrer information
        - Session duration and interactions
        - Anonymous user identifiers (no personal information)

2. How We Use Your Data

The data we collect is used only for:

    Analyzing traffic patterns and securing our servers.
    Optimizing read calls to the Solana network.
    Understanding how users interact with our service to improve user experience.
    Identifying and fixing technical issues.

3. Third-Party Services

We use GDPR-compliant analytics providers hosted in the EU. These providers process data on our behalf and are contractually prohibited from selling or sharing your data with other parties.

4. Cookies and Tracking

We use minimal cookies for:
    - Analytics - to understand usage patterns
    - Essential functionality - to maintain your session

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect site functionality.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
    - Request a copy of your personal data
    - Request correction or deletion of your personal data
    - Opt out of analytics tracking
    - Object to data processing

Contact us at privacy@ivypowered.com to exercise these rights.

6. Data Retention

    Analytics data: Retained according to our analytics provider's policy
    IP addresses: Retained for 30 days
    Cached Solana data: Retained as needed for optimization

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Check this page for the latest version.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@ivypowered.com.
